Our Story

Meet Harish

Hi, I'm Harish. Born in Hassan, Karnataka, I began my photography journey in 2001 out of pure passion. Over 20+ years, I've captured thousands of love stories across Karnataka and beyond. Every click is a vow to freeze time.

At Studio Siddharth, we believe every couple deserves a cinematic love story — told through light, emotion, and artistry. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we craft visual narratives that last generations.
